Katie and Steven

Katie and Steven grew up together in Greenbush, Minnesota. Being classmates in K-12 school, they were friends from the very beginning. Steven clearly remembers having a crush on Katie in kindergarten and keeping a photo of her in his cubby. Through homework sessions and ice skating dates in High School, they began to realize their relationship was special. Now, after dating for 6 and 1/2 years, they are ready to receive the holy sacrament of marriage and enjoy their lives together to the fullest.

Steven and Katie both enjoy outdoor activities and spending time with family. Some of their favorites are fishing, skiing, hiking, camping, and horseback riding. Steven is going to school to receive a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering. Katie is going for a Master's in Food Science and Human Nutrition. One thing they both know is that whatever they end up doing, they want to live in a rural area so they can continue to enjoy their passions.
